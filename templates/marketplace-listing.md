# Marketplace Listing Template 
*Used by marketplace-agent.js as structure reference.* 
 
## PRODUCT HUNT TAGLINE FORMULA 
[Outcome verb] [specific audience] [mechanism or method] 
 
Examples: 
- "Automates weekly marketing for solo founders using GitHub" 
- "Mines Reddit for copy hooks, generates campaigns automatically" 
- "Runs your marketing while you build the product" 
 
Rules: Under 60 chars. Active verb. No adjectives (powerful, amazing, best). 
 
--- 
 
## PRODUCT HUNT FIRST COMMENT (most important PH copy) 
 
**Opening:** The personal "why I built this" (2 sentences, real story) 
**The problem:** Specific, painful, relatable (3 sentences) 
**The solution:** How it works — 3 steps max 
**Who it's for:** Exact ICP statement 
**The differentiator:** One thing no competitor does 
**The ask:** "Would love your feedback on [specific thing]" 
 
Total: 400-500 words 
 
--- 
 
## G2 / CAPTERRA FEATURE BULLETS (10 items) 
 
Rule: Each bullet is SPECIFIC and VERIFIABLE. No vague claims. 
Not: "Powerful AI-driven insights" 
Yes: "Mines top subreddits weekly for verbatim audience pain language"
