# Video Hook Templates 
*For TikTok, Reels, YouTube Shorts. Use as starting points — always adapt to real audience 
language.* 
 
## CONFESSION HOOKS 
"I spent [time] doing [painful thing] until I found [solution category]" 
"Nobody told me [uncomfortable truth] when I started [thing ICP does]" 
"I was embarrassed to admit I was still [manual/inefficient thing] in [year]" 
 
## POV HOOKS 
"POV: you just [specific desirable outcome] in [time]" 
"POV: [ICP role] who finally figured out [painful thing]" 
"POV: [day of week] and [painful thing] is already done" 
 
## NUMBER HOOKS 
"[Specific number] [thing] that [ICP] always gets wrong" 
"[Time] to [outcome]: here's exactly what I did" 
"I tracked [specific metric] for [time period] — here's what I found" 
 
## PATTERN INTERRUPT HOOKS 
"Stop [common thing everyone does] if you want [outcome]" 
"The reason [common approach] doesn't work (and what does)" 
"[Controversial position]: [thing most people believe] is wrong" 
 
## BEFORE/AFTER HOOKS 
"[Specific painful before state] → [specific better after state]" 
"Before: [describe the old painful situation] / After: [describe the new state]" 
 
## HOW TO ADAPT TO REAL LANGUAGE 
1. Take the hook template 
2. Replace [brackets] with exact phrases from copy-ammunition.md 
3. Never use marketing language in brackets — always use what real people say 
4. The hook should sound like something a real person would say, not a template
