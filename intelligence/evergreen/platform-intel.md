## LINKEDIN — 2026 Algorithm Rules 
 
- **No external links anywhere** — confirmed 68% organic reach penalty (links in comments 
also penalized) 
- **Dwell time is the primary signal** — algorithm measures time spent reading, not just 
clicks 
- **Optimal post length: 900-1200 characters** — longer than 1500 drops reach 
- **Declarative hooks outperform questions** — "Most founders get this wrong" beats "Do 
you struggle with X?" 
- **White space increases dwell time** — single-sentence paragraphs scroll better than 
dense blocks 
- **Posting time signal: Tuesday-Thursday 8-10am local time** — LinkedIn feed peaks there 
- **Carousels need PDF upload** — image-only carousels not natively supported 
 
## TWITTER/X — 2026 Algorithm Rules 
 
- **Reply volume in first 60 minutes** is the primary signal (not likes, not retweets) 
- **External links in first tweet** reduce reach — links go in last tweet of thread 
- **Thread length sweet spot: 7-9 tweets** — under 5 = not enough signal, over 12 = drops 
off 
- **Quote tweets are strong positive signal** — controversial takes get quoted, not just 
retweeted 
- **Impressions are vanity** — watch reply rate and quote rate 
 
## TIKTOK — 2026 Algorithm Rules 
 
- **2-second rule is absolute** — if watch time drops below 2 seconds, algorithm suppresses 
- **Completion rate is everything** — optimize for 70%+ completion, not for views 
- **Sound on content outperforms sound off** — 80% of TikTok is watched with sound 
- **Trending audio boosts discovery 30-40%** on TOFU content 
- **#hashtags still work for discovery** — 3-5 niche-specific only 
 
## INSTAGRAM — 2026 Algorithm Rules 
 
- **Reels get 3x organic reach** of static posts 
- **Caption character count: first 125 chars before "more..."** — hook must be in those 125 
- **No external links in captions** — algorithm suppresses posts with URLs 
- **Stories: 5-frame max for optimal completion** 
- **Save rate** is stronger signal than like rate for Reels 
 
## REDDIT — 2026 Rules 
 
- **Bot detection is extremely sensitive** — never use API for posting 
- **Value-first is mandatory** — self-promotion without established karma = immediate 
downvote 
- **Best formats: long-form story with specific result in title** 
- **Top comment after your post** — be the first commenter with additional value 
- **AEO value is confirmed high** — Reddit content is in GPT, Claude, Gemini training data 
 
## NEWSLETTER (Beehiiv) — 2026 Rules 
 
- **Tuesday 10am** recipient's timezone has highest open rates (confirmed 2026) 
- **Subject line: 40-60 characters** optimal for mobile preview 
- **600 words** is optimal length — longer = lower read completion 
- **Plain text outperforms HTML newsletters** for solo founders (feels more personal) 
- **Reply rate is quality signal** — ask a specific question to drive replies
