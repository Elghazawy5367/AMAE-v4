## FORMULA 1 — CONFESSION + SPECIFIC WASTE 
 
**Emotion triggered:** Recognition ("that's exactly me") 
**Best platforms:** LinkedIn, Twitter, TikTok 
**Template:** "I [wasted specific resource] on [common approach] until [turning point]." 
**Example:** "I spent 4 hours writing a LinkedIn post that got 3 likes." 
**Why it works:** Specific numbers + honest failure = instant trust. The reader thinks "if they 
admit that, the rest must be real."
